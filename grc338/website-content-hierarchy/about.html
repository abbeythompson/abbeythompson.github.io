Meet Madelon

Meet the founder of Get Cracked Coffee, Madelon! She was born and raised in the bay area and came to San Luis Obispo for college at California Polytechnic University. While she was in her junior year, she created this wonderful coffee shop, Get Cracked. She started it when she realised how expensive coffee shops priced their coffees, and wanted a more affordable option for college students.

Enjoy your coffee!
